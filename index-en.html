ELASTOS

Blockchain Powered Internet

Elastos is the world's first Internet operating system that uses the Internet as a ground-based infrastructure rather than as an application. It also emphasizes the separation of network communications from application computing, prohibiting applications from directly accessing the network, eliminating most of the virus attacks on the Internet (especially DDoS). Elastos is open source software system, its research and development process by Foxconn and other industrial giants more than two billion yuan sponsorship support, has opened up the source of tens of millions of lines of code, including more than four million lines of original development of the source code See GitHub / Elastos). / Elastos).
